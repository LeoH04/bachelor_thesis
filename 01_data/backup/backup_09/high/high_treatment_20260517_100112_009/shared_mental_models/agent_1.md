## Open Questions and Next‑Step Focus
- Obtain concrete examples of how Candidate C’s communication difficulty and egocentric behavior have affected crew coordination or decision‑making on long‑haul flights.
- Determine whether Candidate C’s lack of willingness to pursue further education poses a tangible risk to adapting to new technology or procedures.

- Assess the real‑world impact of Candidate B’s uncooperative attitude on crew performance and whether targeted training could mitigate it.
- Compare the safety relevance of the repeated teamwork/communication weaknesses across all candidates to prioritize which deficits must be addressed before reaching a final consensus.

## Emerging Group View
- Group‑Leading Candidate: **Candidate B** (supported by all three agents)
- Agreements: All agents agree B has strong technical competence, stress handling, and weather assessment abilities.
- Disagreements: None reported.
- Uncertainties: No data on B’s decision‑making speed or hazard‑anticipation ability; no concrete evidence of B’s teamwork beyond tone issues. Also lacking A’s stress handling and teamwork data, D’s mood stability over long flights, and C’s willingness to develop further.
- Blocking Factors: Absence of evidence for B’s rapid decision making and hazard anticipation, and for A’s stress handling/teamwork, prevents unanimous confidence in any single candidate.

## Open Questions and Next‑Step Focus
- Missing evidence on **Candidate B**: decision‑making speed and hazard‑anticipation capability.
- Missing evidence on **Candidate A**: stress‑handling performance and crew teamwork behavior.
- Need peer or supervisor testimonials on each candidate’s crew interaction under flight conditions.
- Health assessments relevant to long‑haul endurance (especially for A’s eating habits and D’s mood stability).
- Concrete examples of B’s communication style causing operational problems.
- Verification of C’s willingness to pursue further education and its impact on long‑term suitability.
- Confirmation from multiple agents that the above gaps remain unfilled.

## Open Questions and Next-Step Focus
- Missing evidence

- What would change the decision
  - Demonstrated strong teamwork/communication from a candidate with otherwise strong technical profile.
  - Evidence that a candidate’s negative interpersonal traits lead to safety‑relevant incidents.

- What to ask or look for next
  - Peer and crew feedback on each candidate’s collaboration style.
  - Performance records on long‑duration routes.
  - Any training or remediation plans for identified weaknesses.
